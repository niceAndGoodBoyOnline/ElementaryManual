Install elementaryOS


Before starting the installation of elementaryOS ensure you have:

  • A Desktop Computer
  • A bootable elementaryOS USB or DVD

If you do not have a bootable elementaryOS USB or DVD:

Learn how to create a bootable elementaryOS USB

To boot your computer with your bootable elementaryOS USB or DVD:
  1. Turn your desktop computer off.
  2. Insert your bootable elementaryOS USB into a USB port on your desktop computer.
    or
    Insert your bootable elementaryOS DVD into an optical drive on your desktop computer.
  3. Turn your desktop computer on.
  4. Press the bootmenu key on your desktop computer's keyboard
  5. Select your bootable elementaryOS USB or DVD on the bootmenu
  6. For a standard elementaryOS installation, click Yes to every default configuration setting provided.
    OR
    If you want to customize your elementaryOS installation, read this section of the manual.


If you see a prompt to restart your desktop computer, congratulations, you have successfully installed elementaryOS!
