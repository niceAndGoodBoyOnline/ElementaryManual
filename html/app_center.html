How to Use AppCentre


AppCenter allows you to install and manage software for your elementaryOS system.

How to Open AppCentre:
  1. Click on the Application Drawer in the top left corner of your display.
  2. Click on AppCentre.


AppCentre's Home allows you to browse categories of software and search for software.

How to browse Categories for software:
  1. Scroll down the Home panel to see different categories of software.
  2. Click on a category of software.


    3. NOTE: Applications at the top of a Category are Curated Applications. Applications below the "Non-Curated Apps" do not conform to the HIG of elementary OS. They may not function as expected but can be installed.


How to search AppCentre for software:
  1. Click the text field in the top right of AppCentre's Home.
  2. Type a keyword to search AppCentre.
How to Install Software with AppCentre:
  1. Click on a software you want to install.
  2. On details page click the Free button on the top right


NOTE: Many Curated Applications have pay-what-you-want pricing. You may opt out of supporting developers by selecting "$0".

Congratulations, you have installed your first piece of free software!
