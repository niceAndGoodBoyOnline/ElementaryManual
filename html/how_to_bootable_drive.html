How to Make a Bootable USB drive


Downloading Rufus:

  1. Go to the Rufus website.

  2. Scroll down to the "Downloads" section, and select the latest version of Rufus (Rufus 3.8 as of Jan 23, 2020)

Downloading elementaryOS:

  1. Go to the elementary OS website.



  2. In the download section, click on one of the payment amounts.

    3. NOTE: You can click Custom and type "0" in case you don't want to pay.

  3. Click on the Download button.

Formatting the USB drive:

  1. Double click the rufus.exe file that we downloaded in step 1.(It should be in your Downloads folder.)

  2. Select the USB that you want to use under the device drop-down menu.



  3. Click the Select button.



  4. Browse through your files to find the elementary OS ISO that we downloaded earlier. (This should also be in your Downloads folder.)


  5. Click Start.



  6. If any of the following dialog boxes show up, click on the respective buttons marked by the red arrow.


  7. A green 'Ready' bar indicates the process has been completed.


Congratulations! You have successfully created a bootable USB drive!
