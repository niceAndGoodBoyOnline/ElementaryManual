How to Use AppCentre


AppCenter allows you to install and manage software for your elementary OS system.

How to Open AppCentre:
  1. Click on the Application Drawer in the top left corner of your display.



  2. Click on AppCentre.


HOME



AppCentre's Home allows you to browse categories of software and search for software.

How to browse Categories for software:
  1. Scroll down the Home panel to see different categories of software.



  2. Click on a category of software.


    3. NOTE: Applications at the top of a Category are Curated Applications. Applications below the "Non-Curated Apps" do not conform to the HIG of elementary OS. They may not function as expected but can be installed.



How to search AppCentre for software:
  1. Click the text field in the top right of AppCentre's Home.
  2. Type a keyword to search AppCentre.

How to Install Software with AppCentre:
  1. Click on a software you want to install.
  2. On details page click Free on the top right.



  3. Type your user password when prompted for Authentication.
  4. Click on Authenticate or Press Enter.


NOTE: Many Curated Applications have pay-what-you-want pricing. You may opt out of supporting developers by selecting "$0".

Congratulations, you have installed your first elementary OS application!



Installed



AppCentre's Installed allows you to update, review and uninstall software.

How to update elementary OS software with AppCentre:
  1. Click on AppCentre in your Plank or Open in your Application Drawer.
  2. Click on Installed.



  3. Click on Update in the top right.



  4. Type your user password when prompted for Authentication.
  5. Click on Authenticate or Press Enter.




    6. NOTE: By default AppCentre will check for new updates to any software you have installed. When a new update is available AppCentre will appear on your Plank.


How to uninstall software with AppCentre:
  1. Click on AppCentre in your Plank or Open in your Application Drawer.
  2. Click on Installed.
  3. Click on the application you want to uninstall.



  4. Click on uninstall in the top right of the application's details page.
  5. Type your user password when prompted for Authentication.
  6. Click on Authenticate or Press Enter.
    7. NOTE: Some applications, like AppCentre, cannot be uninstalled. This is for your protection.
